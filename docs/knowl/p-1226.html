Notice that these limits give indeterminate forms of the type \(\frac{0}{0}\text{.}\) However, these limits are equal to the limit defintion of \(f'(a)\text{,}\) the derivative of \(f(x)\) at \(x=a\text{.}\) If you can compute \(f'(a)\text{,}\) then you can compute the value of the limit.
