Activity 3.8.5. The box.

Help your company design a box with maximum volume given the following constraints:

  • The box must be made from the following material: an 8 by 8 inches piece of cardboard.

  • To create the box, you are asked to cut the same size square from each corner of the 8 by 8 inches piece of cardboard and to fold the remaining cardboard.

(a)

Draw a diagram illustrating how the box is created.

(b)

Explain why the volume of the box is a function of the side length \(x\) of the cutout squares.

(c)

Express the volume of the box \(V\) as a function of the length of the cuts \(x\text{.}\)

(d)

What is a realistic the domain of this function?

(e)

What cut length \(x\) maximizes the volume of the box?
