The area between the two curves on \([a,b]\) is thus approximated by the Riemann sum

\begin{equation*} A \approx \sum_{i=1}^{n} (f(x_i) - g(x_i)) \Delta x\text{,} \end{equation*}

and as we let \(n \to \infty\text{,}\) it follows that the area is given by the single definite integral

\begin{equation*} A = \int_a^b (f(x) - g(x)) \, dx\text{.} \end{equation*}
