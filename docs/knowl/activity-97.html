Activity 2.5.14.

Use the chain rule and all the rest of the rules you know to find the derivatives of the following functions.

(a)
\begin{equation*} f(x)= -2 \, \sin\left(x^{\frac{7}{4}}\right) \end{equation*}
(b)
\begin{equation*} h(y)= 7 \, e^{\left( -4 \, y^{3} - 2 \, y - 2\right)} \end{equation*}
(c)
\begin{equation*} k(t)= {\left(4 \, t - 6 \, 2^{t} - 2\right)}^{4} \end{equation*}
(d)
\begin{equation*} g(w)= -3 \, \sin\left(w\right)^{\frac{3}{11}} \end{equation*}
